Our Story

About Barrister's Law Academy

Established in 2018, Barrister's Law Academy has swiftly emerged as a trusted name in legal education, offering a comprehensive range of courses for aspiring law professionals.

Under the dynamic leadership of Dr. Priti V. Yadav, a distinguished academician and legal scholar, the Academy is powered by a team of subject experts committed to academic excellence and career-focused mentorship.

Dr. Yadav holds degrees including B.BA.LL.B, LLM, DCL, and PhD in Law, along with various legal certifications. As a university topper and seasoned educator, she brings her experience from reputed institutions such as National Law University, SNDT Women's University, Amity University, and affiliated colleges under Mumbai University.

"Driven by passion, performance, and precision — Barrister's Law Academy continues to shape the next generation of legal minds."

From entrance preparation for MHCET, CLAT, SLAT, and LSAT to in-depth coaching for 3-year LLB and 5-year integrated law programs, the Academy caters to diverse legal education needs. We also provide specialised LLM coaching and competitive exam training including JMFC for Maharashtra Judiciary.
